101 East – Jakarta School Brawl

Home / News / 101 East – Jakarta School Brawl

MAKE Waves is proud to be the producer of the episode ‘Jakarta School Brawl‘ for Al Jazeera’s 101 East series, which will air on 23 March 2017.

On the streets of Jakarta, Indonesia, hundreds of middle and high school students arm themselves with deadly weapons to fight rival gangs from other schools. These brawls can last for hours, leaving blood and bodies in the aftermath.

The violence is extreme, but the motivations are muddy.

In this episode of 101 East, we talk to the teenagers on the frontline, and their parents, teachers and the police about why so many otherwise regular kids are willing to fight and even kill their fellow students.

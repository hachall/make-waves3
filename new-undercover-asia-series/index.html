NEW: Undercover Asia Series

Home / NEW: Undercover Asia Series

Channel NewsAsia’s award-winning investigative series Undercover Asia returns for a third season. This season, we are excited to be able to tell five powerful stories that dig deep into the cracks in Asia’s socio-political fabric – from cybersex crimes to slavery, and even murder.

Two of the episodes have aired on the channel and you can catch them here:

  1. Children of the Cybersex Dens
  2. Murder in Paradise

The third programme, Sextortion Suicide will air tonight, 8th March at 8pm on Channel NewsAsia.

Don’t miss out on the final two episodes we produced airing in the next few weeks. For more information, do refer to the Undercover Asia website here.
